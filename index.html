About This App

This Sorting Visualizer offers an intuitive way to understand how various sorting algorithms work, including Bubble Sort, Merge Sort, Quick Sort, and more. You can customize the array size, sorting speed, and algorithm to suit your learning pace. Additionally, the visualizer provides real-time information on the time complexity of the selected algorithm and the total time taken to complete the sort.
